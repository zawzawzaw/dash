WHAT IS DASH ADVANCE?

Need cash quickly? Dash Advance lets you apply for a personal loan entirely through your mobile phone. No fuss, no muss.

In addition, you’ll enjoy:

Approval in principle in less than 60 seconds

An advance of up to 4x your monthly salary

Interest rates as low as 4.98% p.a.

ARE YOU ELIGIBLE FOR DASH ADVANCE?

Nationality and Minimum Annual Income
  • Singapore Citizen and Singapore Permanent Resident with minimum annual income of S$20,000.
  • Foreigner (with Employment Pass) with minimum annual income of S$60,000.
    • Foreigners must hold P1, P2 or Q type Singapore Employment Passes
    • Q Pass holders must have minimum 1-year validity remaining on their passes
Age Requirement
  • Between 21 to 65 years old.
Mobile Phone Carrier
  • You can sign up for Dash Advance regardless of your telco relationship, all SingTel, Starhub and M1 mobile customers are welcome.
Supporting Documents
  • Sign up on your mobile phone in just 15 mins. You can take photos of your documents required for easy uploading.

# Approval In-Principle Service ("Service") allows you to obtain approval in principle ("AIP") for an online personal loan application. An AIP is a conditional approval that is subject to receipt of complete supporting documents within 30 days and further review by the Bank. The Service is only available from Mon-Sat between 8am-10pm and on Sun and PH between 2-10pm. This Service is available if you apply for the credit facility in your sole name, and you are not an existing principal credit cardholder or personal loan customer of the Bank. If you are an existing principal credit cardholder of the Bank, you are eligible for this Service only if your employment or income details have changed and you submit supporting documents as part of your application.

Standard Chartered Approval In-Principle Service Terms and Conditions apply.
