DASH EASY

Dash Easy is the first solution of its kind in Singapore that allows you to pay friends and businesses directly from your mobile phones. Plus, earn attractive interest rates with a linked Dash Easy Savings Account.

Dash Advance, offered by Standard Chartered Bank (Singapore) Limited, is a seamless way to apply for a loan. In 15 mins, you can apply online or via your mobile phone, which even lets you upload your income documents by taking a photo and get approval-in-principle # within 60 seconds.

DASH ABROAD
