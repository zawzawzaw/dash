WHO WE ARE

Dash is an easy way to manage your money through your mobile phone. With Dash, you don’t have to visit a bank, deal with troublesome paperwork or even go to an ATM. Dash is hassle-free money management at your fingertips.

DASH EASY

Dash Easy is a mobile wallet that makes everyday transactions convenient. Pay friends, cab drivers, or shopkeepers … all through your phone!

DASH ADVANCE

Dash Advance is a personal loan facility, which you can apply for through your phone. Simply upload photos of supporting documents and receive approval in principle in under 60 seconds.

DASH ABROAD

Dash Abroad is a travel insurance plan, which allows you to pick and choose coverage options that best match your needs.
